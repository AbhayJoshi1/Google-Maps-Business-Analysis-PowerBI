# 📍 Google Maps Business Analysis Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes local businesses listed on Google Maps in Chandigarh. The analysis focuses on Cafes, Gyms, and Salons to evaluate their digital presence, customer ratings, review counts, and business opportunities using Microsoft Excel and Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze local business data from Google Maps.
- Compare different business categories.
- Identify businesses with and without websites.
- Evaluate customer ratings and reviews.
- Discover business growth opportunities.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Location:** Chandigarh
- **Business Categories:** Cafes, Gyms, Salons
- **Total Businesses:** 60
- **Data Source:** Google Maps

### Dataset Fields

- Business Name
- Category
- Area
- Rating
- Reviews
- Website (Yes/No)
- Phone
- Address
- Opportunity Level

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Businesses by Category
- Website Distribution
- Average Rating by Category
- Top 10 Businesses by Reviews
- Businesses by Area
- Business Opportunity Level
- Interactive Slicers

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Many businesses do not have a dedicated website, indicating opportunities for digital growth.
- Customer ratings are generally positive across all business categories.
- Businesses with higher review counts demonstrate stronger customer engagement.
- Opportunity analysis helps identify businesses that could benefit from improving their online presence.
